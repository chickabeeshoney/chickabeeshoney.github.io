Our Family Farm Story

What began as a backyard hobby of keeping a few bees has blossomed into a thriving family farm that brings the Oakland community together every Sunday morning.

Our two children are deeply involved in every aspect of the farm - from collecting eggs each morning to helping harvest honey when the season is right. We believe in teaching kids where their food comes from and supporting sustainable farming practices that benefit our local community.

Every Sunday, families gather at our farm for pickup day, complete with complimentary coffee, fresh waffles, and the joy of watching children discover the magic of farm life. It's not just about selling food - it's about building community and creating connections.
