Our Family Farm Story

What started as a backyard beekeeping hobby has grown into a passion for sustainable farming and community connection. Our Oakland farm is home to busy bees, happy chickens, and two curious kids who love sharing the magic of where food comes from.

Every first Sunday of the month, families gather at our farm for pickup day - complete with complimentary coffee, fresh waffles, and the joy of community. It's more than just food; it's about building connections and supporting children's education through our farm programs.
