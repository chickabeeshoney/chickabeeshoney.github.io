Frequently Asked Questions

How many chickens and beehives do you have?

+

We currently have 8 happy chickens and 3 active beehives in our backyard. This gives us plenty of fresh eggs and honey for our monthly boxes while keeping everything manageable for our family operation.

Are there any allergens I should know about?

+

Our products may contain eggs, wheat, and nuts. The sourdough bread contains gluten. All baked goods are made in a kitchen that processes nuts. Please let us know about any allergies when ordering.

How should I store the honey and eggs?

+

Store honey at room temperature in a cool, dry place. Fresh eggs should be refrigerated and will stay fresh for 3-4 weeks. The sourdough bread is best consumed within 3-5 days or can be frozen for longer storage.

Can I customize my box?

+

While we can't customize individual boxes, we offer two different sizes to fit different needs. You can also order individual items like just honey or just eggs if you prefer.

What happens if I miss a pickup?

+

We understand life happens! If you miss a pickup, please contact us via Venmo message. We can usually arrange a alternative pickup time within 24 hours, subject to product freshness.

How do you ensure product quality and safety?

+

We follow all local health guidelines for food production. Our eggs are collected daily and properly washed. Honey is extracted using clean equipment and stored in sterile containers. All baked goods are made fresh within 24 hours of pickup.

Our Story

Chickabees began as a backyard project to teach our boys about ecosystems, gardening, farming, and animal care. What started as a few chickens and beehives has grown into a small business that connects our community with fresh, local food.

Through caring for our chickens and bees, our children learn valuable lessons about responsibility, entrepreneurship, and the importance of sustainable living. All proceeds from Chickabees are invested in their education and future through Roth IRAs, while also reinvesting in our backyard farm to continue growing and improving.

When you support Chickabees, you're not just getting delicious, fresh products – you're investing in the next generation's education and helping sustain a local, family-run operation.

Recipe Corner

Simple recipes using our fresh ingredients

Honey Wheat Bread

A simple, wholesome bread made with our raw honey and fresh ingredients.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup Chickabees honey
  • 1 packet active dry yeast
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 cup warm water

Farm Fresh Egg Frittata

A versatile frittata showcasing the rich flavor of our farm-fresh eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 8 Chickabees fresh eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 cup mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 cup cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Homemade Honey Granola

Crunchy granola sweetened with our pure honey - perfect for breakfast.

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup Chickabees honey
  • 1/2 cup mixed nuts
  • 1/4 cup seeds
  • 1/2 cup dried fruit
  • 1/4 cup coconut oil
